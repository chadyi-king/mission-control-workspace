## Q11: The Planetarium Project
**Question:** What was the Planetarium Project, and what did it turn Earth into?

**Answer:** The Planetarium Project was Vice President Marcus Seneca's initiative to transform Earth into a giant intergalactic spaceship capable of traveling through the stars, uniting all countries for cosmic exploration.

## Q12: The Assassination
**Question:** Who killed Marcus Seneca, and where did it happen?

**Answer:** Marcus Seneca was assassinated by a female sniper with green flames who appeared in the rafters of the UN Global Summit hall. She fired two shots—one hit the ballistic shield protecting President Treagan, and the second shot struck Marcus in the temple as he dove to protect the President.

## Q13: Magic at Birth
**Question:** What did Ryfel do on his first day of life that was "unheard of"?

**Answer:** On his first day of life, Ryfel used earth magic to break out of his crib, shattering stone and clay with his bare hands. His parents called it "unheard of" because no child had ever used magic on the day they were born.

## Q14: The Three Regions
**Question:** Name the three main regions of Crith. Which one has no laws?

**Answer:** The three main regions of Crith are:
1. **Minas-Col** — The kingdom of survivors, hidden behind walls and fog (Ryfel's home)
2. **Runevia** — The largest, most powerful kingdom with a magic-based hierarchy
3. **Despera** — The lawless land between kingdoms with no kings, no rulers, and no laws

**Despera** has no laws.

## Q15: Fire vs Light
**Question:** Which element is described as "most dangerous, hottest, and hardest to control" — Fire or Light?

**Answer:** **Fire** is described as the most dangerous, hottest, and hardest to control element. Once it starts, it spreads and consumes rather than moves.

---

## Q16: How Magic Works
**Question:** Does magic require wands, chanting, or rituals? How DO you use magic?

**Answer:** No, magic does not require wands, chanting, or rituals. Magic is will/intent-based—you simply will it, control it, and command it. It's described as an interaction/listening with the elements, not brute force commanding. Like using your body/muscles, it can tire you from overuse.

---

## Q17: Everyone Has Magic
**Question:** Can all people use all 5 elements, or only some people?

**Answer:** **All people can use all 5 elements.** Everyone has mana (opened by Ether bath at birth) and can use Earth, Water, Air, Light, and Fire. Districts specialize by knowledge and understanding of specific elements, not by birth or caste.

## Q18: Ryfel's Weapon
**Question:** What Earth-style weapon did Ryfel create? How many shots can he fire before it breaks?

**Answer:** Ryfel created a **revolver-style rifle/gun** (called his "little friend from America"). It can fire **1-2 shots per battle** before needing repair, as the technology of this age is still limited.

## Q19: The Cosmic Eye
**Question:** What did Marcus see between death and rebirth?

**Answer:** Marcus saw **a vast, luminous white eye, glowing in the cosmic darkness**—an ancient presence that pulled him toward rebirth. This is referred to as the Cosmic Eye.

## Q20: District Specialization
**Question:** Why is the Blacksmith District good with fire magic?

**Answer:** The Blacksmith District excels at fire magic because they learned to regulate heat for forging weapons. They understand the science of fire and heat control from their craft, making them skilled at managing fire's dangerous, spreading nature.
