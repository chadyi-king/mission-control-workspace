# RE:UNITE Comprehension Test - Answers 21-30

## Q21: Ryfel's Weapons
- **Gauntlets**: Reinforced with retractable hidden blades, grappling hooks, and a revolver-style rifle mechanism; his primary weapons for close combat and utility
- **Grappling Hooks**: Used for mobility, binding enemies, and creating traps
- **Custom Inventions/Gadgets**: Flashbangs, sonic grenades, tear gas, sticky bombs, and elemental magic combinations (earth, fire, wind, water, light)

## Q22: Bram Ironfoot
- **District**: Ironfoot District
- **Fighting Style**: Heavy armor/brute strength tank; wears massive weighted boulder-like armor and moves like a "mountain that moves"; specializes in absorbing hits and dealing crushing blows
- **Secret Ability**: "Fast-Muscle-Fighter-Form" — when he removes his weighted equipment, he reveals a ripped, toned body with incredible speed and power, moving faster than opponents can track
- **Motivation**: To prove Ironfoot strength and be a reliable force for his team; he admires Ryfel's leadership

## Q23: Kriscila Glavni
- **District**: Glavni District (retired Mayor's lineage)
- **Weapons**: Multiple chains wrapped around her arms and waist; uses them for mobility, binding, and striking
- **Medusa Form**: Her chains extend and whip around her at incredible speed like serpents; she travels along the ground slithering like a giant half-snake made of chains, becoming swift and insanely deadly
- **Why She Joins**: She follows Ryfel because he saved her from bullies as a child and taught her how to use magic; she has admired him ever since and wants to stand as his equal

## Q24: Dain Luthren
- **Family Background**: Luthren family — his father Lards and brothers Ham and Tarby were all tournament fighters who served Farmland; however, they abused him during "training," leaving him scarred
- **Fighting Style**: Kamikaze/masochistic brawler with scaled armor that releases shrapnel; his signature move is "Body Detonation" (hugging enemies and releasing shrapnel); uses boxing-style strikes with stone spikes on his knuckles
- **Why He Challenges Ryfel**: Pride and ego — he believed he was the rightful leader due to his family legacy and wanted to prove himself after being outshined; also deeply insecure from his family's abuse

## Q25: Aurex Solmere
- **District**: Magic District
- **Element**: Light (refraction, illusions) combined with water magic for mist effects
- **Why Main Antagonist**: He is the undefeated 3-time Champion of 30 years; represents the pinnacle of the current system; Ryfel's ultimate goal is to defeat him after watching him dominate 10 years ago

## Q26: Ryfel's Fighting Style
Ryfel fights with **tactical innovation and preparation** rather than raw power. He:
- Uses multiple elements (earth, wind, fire, water, light) in combination
- Employs inventions (flashbangs, sonic bombs, grappling hooks, revolver gauntlets)
- Studies opponents extensively and creates counter-strategies
- Uses traps, misdirection, and environmental manipulation
- Combines his Navy SEAL combat experience with magic

## Q27: Team Farmland's Strategy vs Magic District
- **Bram**: Tank/distraction — breaks enemy formations and draws fire with his rock armor; revealed he could reflect light attacks with his teeth
- **Kriscila**: Mobile control — uses chains to bind enemies and disrupt the Magic District's teleportation/illusion tactics
- **Dain**: Kamikaze striker — gets close to enemies and detonates his shrapnel armor
- **Ryfel**: Leader/tactician — observes patterns, directs the team, and creates openings; took one of their mirrors to study their techniques

## Q28: Character Motivations
1. **Ryfel**: To win for Farmland District after watching them lose 10 years ago; to fulfill his 10-year vow
2. **Bram**: To prove Ironfoot strength and be a reliable teammate; also interested in Kriscila romantically
3. **Kriscila**: To prove herself after being bullied for weak magic; to stand beside Ryfel
4. **Dain**: Originally to prove himself as Luthren heir; later to find his own identity beyond his family's abuse

## Q29: The 5 Districts
1. **Farmland District**: Earth magic, agriculture, practical applications; produces food and resources
2. **Blacksmith District**: Fire magic, weapons/armor crafting, heavy combat specialists
3. **Magic District**: Light magic, illusions, tactical manipulation; intellectual approach
4. **Transport District**: Air magic, speed and mobility specialists
5. **Aquaculture District**: Water magic, adaptable fighters who shift between liquid, mist, and ice

## Q30: Ryfel's 10-Year Promise
At age 5, after watching Farmland District come in 3rd place and seeing Aurex Solmere dominate the tournament, Ryfel vowed: **"Ten more years... Ten more fucking years... I will take the stage."** He promised to return in 10 years, win the tournament, and make Farmland the champion.
