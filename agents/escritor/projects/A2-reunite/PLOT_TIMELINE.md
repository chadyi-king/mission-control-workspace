# RE:UNITE Plot Timeline

## Chapter Summaries

### Chapter 1: A World United
**Setting:** Earth (Marcus Seneca's original life)
**Key Events:**
- Marcus Seneca, VP of USA, gives UN speech about "Planetarium Project"
- Transforming Earth into intergalactic spaceship
- Revealed as former Navy SEAL, youngest VP at 39
- Nicknamed "Golden Boy"
- Relationship with Speaker Sofi (sexual/political partnership)
- Assassinated by sniper during speech
- Reincarnated through mysterious white eye in void

**World Building:**
- Earth unified under space exploration goal
- East Timor and Laos still resisting unification
- Amethyst minerals valuable for spacecraft
- Politics and personal life intertwined

---

### Chapter 2: To A New World
**Setting:** Crith (Rebirth)
**Key Events:**
- Marcus reborn as Ryfel Redimir
- Ether Ritual performed (newborn bath with glowing turquoise liquid)
- Parents: Ryker (enthusiastic farmer/noble) and Elda (sharp political wife)
- Servant Milal introduced
- Redimir estate described (farmland, manor, noble status)
- Ryfel's internal panic at being baby with adult mind
- Falls asleep from exhaustion

**World Building:**
- Ether opens Mana Pores in newborns
- Minas-Col kingdom
- Farmland District
- House Redimir: Counts of Farmland, agriculture-focused

---

### Chapter 3: History, Magic and His Place
**Setting:** Ryfel age 1-5
**Key Events:**
- Ryker discovers Ryfel used magic at 1 day old (broke crib)
- Parents spin in celebration (family ritual)
- Language learning through immersion
- History lesson: Crith's three kingdoms
  - Minas-Col (survivors, walled kingdom)
  - Despera (lawless lands)
  - Runevia (magic kingdom, conquerors)
- First magic lesson: Five elements explained
- Ryfel creates aurora using all elements (age 5)
- Declared "Contender" for district

**World Building:**
- Five elements: Earth, Water, Fire, Air, Light
- Contender Tournament explained
- Magic system basics
- District structure of Minas-Col
- Runevia threat established

### Chapter 9: The Contender Tournament
**Setting:** Tournament begins
**Key Events:**

- Ryfel joins them, defeats Blacksmith team

**Combat Highlights:**

### Chapter 11: The Champion
**Setting:** Final matches
**Key Events:**
- Ryfel vs Bram (match completion)
- Ryfel vs Aurex Solmere (FINAL MATCH)
  - Intense multi-phase battle
  - Aurex's illusions vs Ryfel's counters
  - Ryfel uses revolver gauntlets
  - Bare-handed final combat
  - Ryfel defeats Aurex
- Becomes new Champion
- Festival celebration announced

**Combat Highlights:**
- Greatest battle in series so far
- Aurex's techniques: Mist, invisibility, light attacks
- Ryfel's innovations: Wind clearing, traps, revolver
- Final fist fight - respect between warriors
- "I Fucked Around enough, time to Find Out"

### Chapter 12: The Path Forward
**Setting:** Farmland Festival / Attack
**Key Events:**
**Part 1: Festival**
- Victory celebration
- Elda and Milal reveal pregnancies
- Kriscila confesses feelings (drunk)
- 3-year promise made
- First kiss between Ryfel and Kriscila
- Dain's family reconciliation

**Part 2: The Attack**
- Magic Knights from Runevia breach wall
- 150 knights attack festival
- Massacre: 1000+ killed, 300 captured
- Grandpa Ironfoot dies protecting Bram
- Dain dies protecting his family
- Aurex dies protecting Lux (hidden in woods)
- Ryker's leg severed by Knight Commander
- Milal knocked unconscious

**Part 3: Ryfel's Last Stand**
- Ryfel fights 30+ knights
- Kills 15+ using full arsenal
- Faces Knight Commander
- Severes Commander's arm
- Stopped when Commander takes Ryker hostage
- Captured along with Bram
- Taken to Runevia as prisoners

**Cliffhanger:**
- Ryfel and Bram captured
- Ryker severely injured
- Dain and Aurex dead
- Many others dead or captured
- War has begun
