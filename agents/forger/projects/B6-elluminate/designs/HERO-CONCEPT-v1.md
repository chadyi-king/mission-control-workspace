## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Figures (Illustration Style)
**Option A: Modern Flat Illustrations**
- Clean, geometric character design (not clip-art)
- Consistent art style across all three
- Professional but approachable
- Slightly abstracted (not photorealistic)

**Option B: Silhouette + Accent**
- Dark silhouettes with colored lightbulb "glow" effect
- More sophisticated, premium feel
- Focus on the SPARK metaphor

### Lightbulbs (The "Spark")
- Glowing effect with radial gradient
- Colors: Blue (#4A90D9), Orange (#F4A261), Green (#2E5C4F)
- Subtle animation potential (gentle pulse)

### Typography
```

"IGNITE THE SPARK" - 4rem desktop, 2.5rem mobile
"WITHIN YOUR TEAM" - 3rem desktop, 2rem mobile
```
