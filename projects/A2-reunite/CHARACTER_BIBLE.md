# RE:UNITE Character Bible

## Main Characters

### Ryfel Redimir (Marcus Seneca)
**Role:** Protagonist, Contender Champion

**Background:**
- Reincarnated from Marcus Seneca, former US Vice President and Navy SEAL
- Died at 39 from sniper assassination during UN Global Summit
- Reborn as Ryfel Redimir in Minas-Col kingdom's Farmland District

**Physical Appearance:**
- Age 15 (at tournament)
- Dark hair with golden streak (from parents)
- Emerald green eyes
- Lean, chiseled build from SEAL training + magic conditioning
- Wears reinforced gauntlets with House Redimir insignia

**Personality:**
- Strategic, analytical thinker (OODA loop mentality)
- Protective of family (core motivation)
- Innovative - merges Earth tactics with Crith magic
- Precise speech, measured words
- Internal monologue references SEAL training and political scenarios
- Independent but respectful to authority

**Abilities:**
- Multi-elemental magic mastery (Earth, Water, Fire, Air, Light)
- SEAL tactical combat
- Inventor (revolver gauntlets, traps, grenades, flashbangs)
- Trap-based warfare philosophy ("traps never fail")
- Scientific approach to magic

**Relationships:**
- **Ryker (Father):** Respects his enthusiasm, finds him overdramatic, wants to make him proud
- **Elda (Mother):** Close bond, protective of her
- **Milal (Servant/Mother figure):** Friendly, food-loving companion
- **Kriscila (Love interest):** 3-year promise, protective, romantic tension
- **Bram (Teammate/Friend):** Loyal tank, comic relief, captured with him
- **Dain (Teammate):** Rival turned ally, died protecting family

**Voice Patterns:**
- Precise, strategic questions
- Internal: Analytical with SEAL references
- Combat: Confident, taunting opponents
- Family: Warm but measured

---

### Ryker Redimir
**Role:** Ryfel's father, Lord of Farmland District, Head of Outer Courts

**Physical Appearance:**
- Broad-shouldered, muscular (farmer build)
- Dark hair
- Earth-stained hands
- Wears sleeveless farming tunic + formal lord attire when needed

**Personality:**
- Enthusiastic, dramatic, loud
- "Dad joke" energy but competent
- Protective father
- Proud of son to excess
- Boisterous laughter
- Passionate about Farmland District

**Speech Patterns:**
- Exclamation-heavy
- Repeats names for emphasis ("RYYY-KERRR!")
- "Little Ry" for Ryfel
- Boisterous announcements
- Uses farming metaphors

**Abilities:**
- Earth magic mastery
- Farming techniques applied to combat
- Leadership of district
- Can shift between noble and farmer personas

**Relationships:**
- **Elda:** Loving wife, teases her constantly
- **Milal:** Co-parent (polygamous relationship normal in world)
- **Ryfel:** Extreme paternal pride, sees himself in son

---

### Elda Redimir
**Role:** Ryfel's mother, Lady of Farmland District

**Physical Appearance:**
- Auburn hair
- Elegant but practical
- Currently pregnant (3 months at Chapter 12)
- Wears earth-tone noble attire

**Personality:**
- Sharp, politically aware
- Sighs often at Ryker's antics
- Softens with genuine emotion
- Strategic thinker like Ryfel
- Protective mother (hates tournament violence)

**Speech Patterns:**
- Measured, thoughtful
- Dry humor
- Questions Ryker's dramatics
- Soft voice with Ryfel
- Worried tone about safety

**Abilities:**
- Water magic specialist
- Political maneuvering
- Household management
- Keeps Ryker grounded

**Relationships:**
- **Ryker:** Loving but exasperated wife
- **Ryfel:** Overprotective, sees his genius
- **Milal:** Accepts as co-parent
- **Kriscila:** Approves of relationship with Ryfel

### Milal
**Role:** Servant, Ryfel's caretaker, co-mother to new child

**Physical Appearance:**
- Auburn hair, bright green eyes
- Practical maid uniform (switches to ninja armor in combat)
- Revealed to be skilled fighter

**Personality:**
- Casual, friendly with family
- Food-obsessed (132 skewer record)
- Supportive of Ryfel
- Gossipy but loyal
- Hidden combat skills (ninja-like)

**Speech Patterns:**
- Informal with family
- Exclaims about food
- "Young Master" for Ryfel
- Casual teasing
- Enthusiastic cheering

**Abilities:**
- Housework magic
- Combat: Needles, skewers, ninja armor
- 132-skewer eating capacity
- Protective instincts

**Relationships:**
- **Ryfel:** Devoted caretaker, treats as family
- **Ryker/Elda:** Close, like family
- **Kriscila:** Matchmaking, teasing

### Kriscila Glavni
**Role:** Teammate, love interest

**Physical Appearance:**
- Platinum-silver hair
- Violet eyes
- Athletic build
- Wears chain weapons wrapped around body
- "Medusa made of chains" appearance

**Personality:**
- Public: Fierce warrior, sharp tongue, cold
- Private: Shy, vulnerable with Ryfel
- Determined, prideful
- Struggles with expressing emotions

**Speech Patterns:**
- Public: Sharp, confrontational
- With Ryfel: Stammers, softens
- Drunk (Chapter 12): Confession, giggling
- Combat: Commands, observations

**Abilities:**
- Chain weapons mastery
- Earth magic
- Close combat specialist
- Can entangle/disarm opponents

**Relationships:**
- **Ryfel:** Childhood crush (since he saved her from bullies), 3-year promise, deeply in love
- **Dain:** Former bully, tense partnership, mutual respect by end
- **Bram:** Teammate, not romantically interested

**Key Moments:**
- Childhood: Bullied by Dain's group, saved by Ryfel
- Training: Struggled with magic until Ryfel taught her
- Confession: Drunken admission at festival (Chapter 12)
- Promise: 3-year agreement to be together when adults

### Bram Ironfoot
**Role:** Teammate, tank, comic relief

**Physical Appearance:**
- Lean but muscular
- Sun-darkened skin
- Boulder-like armor (slow but durable)
- Grinning, friendly face

**Personality:**
- Laid-back, casual
- Food-loving
- Loyal friend
- Comic relief
- Brave in battle
- Protective of teammates

**Speech Patterns:**
- Casual, friendly
- Food references
- "He he heh" laughter
- Combat enthusiasm
- Teasing humor

**Abilities:**
- Earth armor (boulder-like plating)
- Raw strength
- Speed when unarmored
- "Here's Brammy!" charge attack
- Can fight without armor (rage mode after Grandpa dies)

**Relationships:**
- **Ryfel:** Loyal teammate, respects his strategy
- **Kriscila:** Flirtatious but unreciprocated
- **Grandpa Ironfoot:** Deep bond, devastated by his death
- **Dain:** Friendly rivalry

**Status at Chapter 12 End:** Captured by Magic Knights

### Dain Luthren
**Role:** Teammate (deceased Chapter 12)

**Physical Appearance:**
- Cheese-grater armor design
- Kamikaze fighter build
- Sharp features

**Personality:**
- Initially: Arrogant, bully, desperate to prove himself
- Later: Redeemed, self-sacrificing
- Troubled family history (abusive "training")
- Wanted to lead but learned to follow

**Speech Patterns:**
- Initially mocking, aggressive
- Later: Respectful, supportive
- "Masochistic grin" in combat
- Names his attacks

**Abilities:**
- Self-damaging attacks (Body Detonation)
- Earth magic
- Kamikaze fighting style
- Endurance-based

**Relationships:**
- **Ryfel:** Rival → respected leader
- **Kriscila:** Former bully → teammate
- **Family (Lards, Ham, Tarby):** Abused by them, died saving them

**Death:** Chapter 12 - Sacrificed himself to save his family from Magic Knights

### Aurex Solmere (Deceased)
**Role:** Former Champion, antagonist turned ally/mentor figure

**Physical Appearance:**
- Tall, lean but powerful
- Silver hair, tied back
- Piercing gold eyes
- Sleek armor that shimmers with otherworldly light
- Uses twin-bladed spear "Swallow"

**Personality:**
- Noble warrior
- Respected opponent
- Loves his daughter deeply
- 30-year undefeated champion
- Humble in defeat

**Abilities:**
- Light magic mastery
- Illusion creation (Spectacle of Ghosts)
- Silver Mist invisibility technique
- Swallow-shaped light projectiles
- 40 years of combat experience

**Status:** Died Chapter 12 protecting daughter Lux

---

### Lux Solmere
**Role:** Aurex's daughter, "Bright Star"

**Physical Appearance:**
- Silver hair, gold eyes
- Similar appearance to father
- Young (same age as Ryfel)

**Personality:**
- Talented performer and fighter
- Ambitious
- Loved her father deeply
- Strategic thinker (suggested alliance with Farmland)
- Grieving at Chapter 12 end

**Abilities:**
- Light magic
- Illusion techniques
- Performer skills
- Strong fighter (was training to beat her father)

**Status:** Orphaned at Chapter 12 end; witnessed father's death; hidden in woods

## Minor Characters

### Grandpa Ironfoot (Deceased)
- Bram's grandfather
- Gritty veteran, proud of Ryfel
- Died protecting Bram in Chapter 12
- "Hmph... smart move, boy" - approval of Ryfel

- Dain's father and brothers
- Abused Dain under "training" guise
- Reconciled with Dain before his death
- Escaped thanks to Dain's sacrifice

### Varian Draganov
- Lord of Blacksmith District
- Father of Garrik, Viktor, Tomas
- Arrogant, looks down on Farmland
- Mocked by Ryker in political exchange

### Garrik, Viktor, Tomas Draganov
- Blacksmith District fighters
- Garrik: Strongest, arrogant
- Viktor: Tactician
- Tomas: Reckless, aggressive
- Defeated by Ryfel's team
