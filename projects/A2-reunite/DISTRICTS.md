# RE:UNITE — Districts of Minas-Col
**World:** Crith  
**Kingdom:** Minas-Col  
**File Purpose:** Quick reference for district specializations

---

## THE 5 DISTRICTS

### 1. FARMLAND DISTRICT
- **Element:** Earth
- **Specialization:** Agriculture, soil/stone manipulation
- **Knowledge:** Different soils, minerals, stones, cultivation
- **House:** Redimir (Count Ryker Redimir)
- **Notable Character:** Ryfel Redimir (Champion)
- **Culture:** Practical, hardworking, "farmer-nobles"
- **Status:** Now leads Outer Courts after Ryfel's victory

### 2. BLACKSMITH DISTRICT
- **Element:** Fire
- **Specialization:** Weapons/armor crafting, heat regulation
- **Knowledge:** Fire control for forging, metallurgy
- **House:** Draganov (Lord Varian Draganov)
- **Notable Characters:** Garrik, Viktor, Tomas (Draganov sons)
- **Culture:** Warrior-focused, proud, aggressive
- **Fighting Style:** Heavy weapons + fire magic

### 3. MAGIC DISTRICT
- **Element:** Light
- **Specialization:** Illusions, refraction, tactical manipulation
- **Knowledge:** Light physics (moves in straight lines), bending light
- **House:** Solmere (Aurex Solmere — former Champion)
- **Notable Characters:** Aurex, Lux Solmere
- **Culture:** Intellectual, noble-focused, status-conscious
- **Fighting Style:** Illusions + light projectiles + misdirection

### 4. TRANSPORT DISTRICT
- **Element:** Air
- **Specialization:** Speed, mobility
- **Knowledge:** Wind patterns, air currents
- **Fighting Style:** Fast movement, hit-and-run
- **Note:** Defeated by Blacksmith in early tournament rounds

### 5. AQUACULTURE DISTRICT
- **Element:** Water
- **Specialization:** Adaptable fluid combat
- **Knowledge:** Water states (liquid, mist, ice)
- **Fighting Style:** Shifting between forms, unpredictable
- **Note:** Defeated by Blacksmith in early tournament rounds

---

## KEY DISTRICT DYNAMICS

**Farmland vs Magic District:**
- Historic rivalry — Magic looked down on "farmers"
- Ryfel's victory upends the hierarchy
- Aurex now respects Ryfel after defeat

**Blacksmith District:**
- Aggressive expansionists
- Varian and sons (Garrik, Viktor, Tomas) are powerful but arrogant
- Defeated by Ryfel in tournament

---

## OUTER COURTS LEADERSHIP
**What Head of Outer Courts Controls:**
1. District governance — policy for all 5 districts
2. Military coordination — combined district defenses
3. Resource allocation — trade and distribution
4. Tournament oversight — host/set terms for Contender Tournament
