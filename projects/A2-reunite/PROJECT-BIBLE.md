# RE:UNITE — Story Bible

## Overview
- **Genre:** Isekai / Fantasy / Political Intrigue
- **Premise:** Vice President Marcus Seneca dies on Earth and is reborn as Ryfel in a magical world
- **Target:** Young Adult/Adult (contains mature content)
- **Status:** 12 chapters complete, Chapter 13 in progress

---

## Protagonist

### Marcus Seneca / Ryfel Redimir

**Original Identity (Earth):**
- Name: Marcus Seneca
- Title: "Golden Boy"
- Position: Vice President of the United States
- Age: 39 (youngest VP in US history)
- Background: Navy SEALs veteran
- Leader of "Planetarium Project" — transformed Earth into a spaceship

**Reborn Identity (New World):**
- Name: Ryfel Redimir
- Family: House Redimir (noble family)
- Parents: Ryker (father), Elda (mother)
- Servant: Milal
- Age: 5 years old (Chapter 4+), born with adult consciousness

**Key Traits:**
- Genius-level intellect
- Born with magic (extremely rare — same-day magic use)
- Super strength from birth (crushed stone/clay as newborn)
- Former Navy SEAL training + political acumen
- Hides his true abilities, trains in secret
- Uses Tai Chi for mana circulation

## World Setting: Minas-Col

### Geography & Structure
- **Kingdom:** Minas-Col
- **Districts:**
  - Magic District (enchanted cloaks, spellcasters)
  - Blacksmith District (weaponry, armor)
  - Outer Courts (various regions)
- **House Redimir:** Controls agriculture, respected but not feared politically

### Magic System: Ether
- **Terminology:** Magic is called "Ether"
- **Elemental Types:**
  - Earth magic (Ryfel's primary)
  - Air magic
  - Water magic
  - Fire magic (sparks, lighting)
- **Usage:**
  - Mana flows beneath the skin
  - Requires "feel and flow" control
  - Can be cast through gestures, thoughts
- ** rarity:** Using magic on the day of birth is unheard of (divine blessing level)

### The Contender Tournament
- **Purpose:** Test strength, intellect, and leadership
- **Frequency:** Decennial (every 10 years)
- **Rewards:**
  - Winners shape kingdom's progress for next decade
  - Control resource distribution
  - Influence policy-making
  - Prioritize advancements
- **Participants:** Warriors and minds from Outer Courts
- **Not just fighting:** About winning hearts and influence

## Plot Arc Summary

### Phase 1: Death & Rebirth (Chapters 1-3)
- Marcus assassinated (sniper) after UN speech
- Dies, sees cosmic eye
- Reborn as Ryfel with full memories
- Discovers he has magic from birth

### Phase 2: Hidden Growth (Chapter 4)
- Age 5, secretly training
- Morning routine: Tai Chi, elemental practice
- Reading noble history, magic theory
- Pretends to be normal child

### Phase 3: The Tournament (Chapters 6+)
- Family attends Contender Tournament
- Ryfel learns about power structure
- Sets sights on future participation

## Writing Style Notes

### Tone
- Mix of serious political intrigue and comedy
- Dramatic character reactions
- Internal monologues in parentheses
- Action scenes with detailed descriptions

### Dialogue Style
- Character name + emotion in parentheses
- Example: "Ryker (grinning): 'That's my boy!'"
- Thoughts labeled clearly

### Magic Descriptions
- Sensory details (viscous substance, mana flow)
- Elemental associations
- Physical sensations of casting

### Pacing
- Fast transitions between scenes
- Time jumps (5 years in Chapter 4)
- Mix of slice-of-life and action
