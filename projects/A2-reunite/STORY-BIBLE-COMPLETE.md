# RE:UNITE — Complete Story Bible & Agent Memory

## Story Concept
An isekai political fantasy where a US Vice President is reborn into a world of elemental magic, using his past life's strategic brilliance to revolutionize magical combat and climb the ranks of a rigid caste system.

---

## Protagonist

### Marcus Seneca → Ryfel Redimir

**Original Identity (Earth):**
- **Name:** Marcus Seneca
- **Title:** "Golden Boy"
- **Position:** Vice President of the United States (youngest in history at 39)
- **Background:** Navy SEALs veteran → Politics
- **Leadership:** Led "Planetarium Project" — transformed Earth into an intergalactic spaceship
- **Death:** Assassinated by sniper during UN Global Summit speech
- **Personality:** Charismatic, strategic, natural leader, protective

**Reborn Identity (Crith):**
- **Name:** Ryfel Redimir  
- **House:** Redimir (noble family, agriculture-focused)
- **Parents:** Ryker (father, enthusiastic farmer), Elda (mother, sharp & politically aware)
- **Servant:** Milal (loyal, food-loving, supportive)
- **Age Timeline:**
  - Chapter 1-3: Newborn to 1 year
  - Chapter 4: 5 years old
  - Chapter 6-12: Contender age (appears to be pre-teen/teen)

**Core Characteristics:**
- **Genius-level intellect** from previous life
- **Navy SEAL training** → Applied to magic combat
- **Political acumen** → Navigates noble politics
- **Discoverer, not hider:** Explores magic system openly (with family)
- **Strategic innovator:** Merges Earth's tactics with Crith's magic

## World Setting: Crith

### Geography & Kingdoms
**Three Dominant Kingdoms:**
1. **Minas-Col** — Home kingdom, agricultural focus, Outer Courts system
2. **Runevia** — Magic-obsessed kingdom, caste system based on power
3. **Kingdom of Light** — Mentioned ally/enemy

### Districts of Minas-Col (Outer Courts System)
- **Farmland District** — Agriculture, House Redimir's domain
- **Magic District** — Enchanted cloaks, spellcasters
- **Blacksmith District** — Weapons, armor, warriors
- **Five Districts total** — Each represented in Contender Tournament

### Social Structure
- **Noble Houses:** Control districts, compete for Outer Courts leadership
- **Contender System:** Tournament determines resource allocation for 10 years
- **Runevia Caste:** Magic power = social rank (contrasts with Minas-Col)

## Magic System: The Five Elements

### Core Elements (Standard)
1. **Earth** — Defense, structure, terrain manipulation
2. **Water** — Healing, fluidity, adaptability  
3. **Fire** — Offense, heat, destruction
4. **Air** — Speed, mobility, breath control

### Fifth Element (Rare)
5. **LIGHT** — Illusions, purification, energy, truth-revealing
   - Mentioned in Chapter 6: Flags of "Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Light"
   - Aurex Solmere appears to use light magic
   - Associated with higher-tier magic users

### Magic Mechanics
- **Source:** Ether (ambient magical energy)
- **Internal:** Mana flows beneath skin
- **Casting:** Gestures, breath control, thought
- **Training:** Feel and flow of mana, Tai Chi-like movements
- **Discovery:** Ryfel experiments systematically, applies physics knowledge

### Ryfel's Magic Style
- **Multi-elemental:** Uses all 4 basic elements seamlessly
- **Tactical application:** Navy SEAL mindset — efficiency over flashiness
- **Innovation:** Creates new spell combinations (traps, movement techniques)
- **Hidden depth:** Doesn't reveal full capabilities, always holds reserve

## Writing Style Guide

### Narrative Style
- **Third person limited** — Stays in Ryfel's perspective
- **Internal monologue** — Heavy use of Ryfel's thoughts
- **Tone shifts:** Serious political/strategic ↔ Comedy/family moments

### Dialogue Format
```
Character (emotion/descriptor): "Dialogue text"
Example: Ryker (grinning): "That's my boy!"
```

### Action Scenes
- Detailed sensory descriptions
- Magic described through physical sensation
- Strategic analysis during combat
- Multi-elemental combinations emphasized

### Comedy Elements
- Ryfel's internal frustration with baby limitations
- Ryker's over-the-top enthusiasm
- Family spinning celebration ritual
- Milal's food obsession

### Chapter Structure
- Opening with scene-setting (atmosphere, sensory)
- Character interaction/dialogue
- Strategic revelation or training
- Climactic moment (combat, discovery, or emotional beat)
- Closing hook for next chapter

## Agent Instructions for Writing

### When Writing Ryfel:
1. **Strategic mindset first** — He analyzes before acting
2. **Multi-elemental magic** — Combine elements creatively
3. **Navy SEAL references** — Tactics, discipline, training
4. **Protective of family** — Core motivation
5. **Innovation** — Create new techniques, don't just copy

### Tone Balance:
- 70% serious/strategic/political
- 30% comedy/family warmth
- Never fully grimdark, never fully silly

### Magic Writing:
- Describe physical sensation of mana
- Show tactical application (not just power levels)
- Emphasize creative combinations
- Include training/learning moments

### Dialogue Rules:
- Use (emotion) tags for clarity
- Ryker: Loud, enthusiastic, physical
- Elda: Sharp, measured, caring
- Ryfel: Precise, strategic, respectful but independent
