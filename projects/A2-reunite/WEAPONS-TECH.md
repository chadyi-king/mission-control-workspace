## RYFEL'S INVENTIONS

### 1. REVOLVER-STYLE GAUNTLET RIFLE
**What it is:** Hidden gun mechanism in wrist gauntlet
**How it works:** 
- Barrel extends from under wrist
- Fires bullets made of compressed earth, metal, and fire magic
- Manual rotation between shots (turn barrel by hand)
**Limitations:** 
- Only 1-2 shots per battle (limited by technology of the age)
- Needs repair after use
- Technology can't be mass-produced yet

### 2. FLASHBANG GRENADES
**Effect:** Blinding white light + deafening sound
**Usage:** Disorients enemies, breaks concentration
**Used against:** Air Fighters, Bram

### 3. SONIC GRENADES/MINES
**Effect:** High-frequency sonic blasts
**Damage:** Hurts hearing, causes disorientation
**Used against:** Water Leader

### 4. STICKY FRAG GRENADES
**Effect:** Adhesive bombs that stick to targets before exploding
**Usage:** Traps enemies, guaranteed hit
**Used against:** Blacksmith District

### 5. TEAR GAS GRENADES
**Effect:** Stinging smoke overwhelming senses
**Usage:** Disables enemies without killing
**Used against:** Viktor (Draganov)

### 6. GRAPPLING HOOKS
**Built into:** Gauntlets
**Uses:** 
- Mobility (swing across battlefield)
- Trapping opponents (bind limbs)
- Creating mid-air combat opportunities
- Retrieving items

### 7. HIDDEN BLADES
**Built into:** Gauntlets
**Usage:** Close combat surprise attacks
