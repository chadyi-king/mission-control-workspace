## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Re:Unite** is a fantasy/isekai narrative that follows Marcus Seneca, a 39-year-old Vice President of the United States, who is assassinated while saving the President and reincarnates as Ryfel Redimir in a magical world called **Crith**. The story blends political thriller elements with classic isekai tropes, featuring a mature protagonist navigating a new world with his memories intact.

**Genre:** Isekai, Political Fantasy, Magic System
**Target Audience:** Adult (mature themes, sexual content, violence)
**Tone:** Mix of serious political drama, action, and occasional levity

## III. MAGIC SYSTEM

### Core Mechanic: Ether/Mana

**Ether:**
- Glowing turquoise liquid used in birth rituals
- Creates "connection" between infant and "world's mana"
- Applied externally but seeps into pores
- Causes "faint glow" upon contact with skin

**Key Ritual - The Ether Ritual:**
- Performed immediately after birth
- Uses "sealed vial filled with glowing turquoise liquid"
- Administered by priest in sacred context
- "Room was immediately filled with a soft, soothing energy"
- Purpose: "link between himself and the world's mana"

### Observed Magical Effects

**1. Green Flame Magic (Assassin):**
- Used for transportation: "green flame ignited on the floor"
- Portal/teleportation: "two dark, slender hands reached through"
- Weapon creation: "sniper rifle materialized from fire"
- Self-targeting: Flames "never consumed her skin-tight suit"
- Exit mechanism: "body sank into it, vanishing into the fire"

**2. Purple Barrier (Marcus's Ring):**
- Protective energy field activated during assassination attempt
- "Soft purple glow around him" when shot explodes glass
- Disappears after impact
- Connected to "promise ring" between Marcus and Don

**3. Emerald Flames (Cursed/Dying):**
- Ignited from bullet wound in Marcus's head
- Consumed only the victim ("weren't burning anything else—just Marcus")
- Spread rapidly: "burning too fast—too aggressively"
- Blackened and destroyed body
- Left only "small tuft of golden hair" untouched

**4. Earth Magic (Ryker):**
- Earthen locks respond to palm
- Can shape earth: "earth beneath them shifted, forming a smooth ramp"
- Musical/mechanical clicking suggests precise control

### System Limitations & Rules

From observed instances:
- Magic requires specific conditions (ritual for Ether connection)
- Some magic is destructive and uncontrollable (emerald flames)
- Magic items have limited uses (assassin: "looks like this is all I can conjure up in this place")
- Protective magic exists but may be single-use

### Cosmic/Transmigration Elements

**Afterlife/Transmigration:**
- "Endless void" with "ocean of stars"
- Cosmic eye entity that judges/interacts with souls
- Sensation of swimming in "viscous substance"
- Force that pulls soul toward new existence
- Awareness maintained through transition (Marcus keeps memories)
