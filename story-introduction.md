# 🐧 THE LAST COLONY: A Penguin Survival Challenge

---

## 📖 Story: The Ice is Breaking

**The old world is gone.**

Rising temperatures have shattered the ancient ice kingdoms. Your colony's homeland - the ice they've known for generations - is melting beneath their feet. As brave explorers and survivors, you are the **First Penguins**, venturing beyond the ruins to establish a new home.

But you arrive with almost nothing.

Scattered across this unfamiliar landscape are remnants of the human world: **straws, ice cream sticks, tape, bottle caps, cardboard pieces** - discarded materials that will become your lifeline. It is up to **you** to gather these recycled materials and rebuild a sustainable penguin city from scratch.

This is your chance to embody the legendary **"1st Penguin Spirit"** - the courage to leap into the unknown when no one else will, to build something extraordinary from nothing, and to forge a future where others see only ruins.

## ⚠️ The Challenges Ahead

**Survival won't come easy.**

As you build, **crises will strike without warning**:
- 🌊 **Environmental Disasters** - Floods, storms, and ice shifts threaten your structures
- 🏗️ **Structural Failures** - Buildings may collapse under pressure
- 📦 **Resource Shortages** - Materials will run scarce when you need them most
- ⏰ **Time Pressure** - The ice is melting faster than you think

Each challenge will test your creativity, teamwork, and resilience. How will your colony respond when disaster strikes? Will you adapt, improvise, and overcome?

## 🎯 Your Mission

**Build. Adapt. Survive.**

Show the world what the human spirit - and penguin determination - can achieve.

**The ice is melting. The clock is ticking. Your colony awaits.**

# 🎮 GAME INSTRUCTIONS

## Phase 1: The Build (60 minutes)

Your team must construct a mini penguin city using **only recycled materials**:
- Straws and ice cream sticks
- Tape and glue
- Bottle caps and cardboard
- Any other discarded items you can find

**Requirements:**
- Build at least 3 structures (homes, towers, bridges, etc.)
- Create space for your penguin figurines to "live"
- Make it stable enough to withstand... **challenges**

## Phase 2: The Crises (Throughout)

The Game Master will announce **random crises** during your build:

**Example Crises:**
- 🌊 **FLOOD WARNING!** - All structures must survive a water test
- 💨 **BLIZZARD!** - Can your buildings withstand wind?
- 🔥 **HEAT WAVE!** - Materials start melting/sagging
- 🏴‍☠️ **RESOURCE RAID!** - Lose 50% of your materials
- 🌍 **EARTHQUAKE!** - Shake test for stability

**How to Survive:**
- Adapt your designs quickly
- Use teamwork to reinforce structures
- Improvise with limited resources

## Phase 3: The Final Test (10 minutes)

The ultimate challenge: **The Melting Test**

Your completed city must survive a series of environmental disasters. The colony with the most structures still standing wins!

## 🏆 Victory Conditions

- **Survival** - Most structures intact after all crises
- **Creativity** - Most innovative use of recycled materials
- **Teamwork** - Best collaboration and communication
- **1st Penguin Spirit** - Showed courage, adaptability, and resilience

## 📦 Materials Provided

- Straws (various sizes)
- Ice cream sticks / popsicle sticks
- Masking tape and double-sided tape
- Scissors and glue
- Cardboard pieces
- Bottle caps
- **Bonus:** Mystery recyclables (revealed during game!)
